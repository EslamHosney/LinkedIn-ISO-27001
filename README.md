## Clause 7
- 7.1 Resources
  - Provide the right resources for your ISMS
- 7.2 Competence
  - of people based on education, training or experience.
  - Records include evidence of education, training and experience 
  - Recruit competent personnel, provide access to education and encourage people to self-study
- 7.3 Awareness
  - Everyone must be aware of the information security policy, their contribution to the IS policy and the implications of not conforming to IS reqs
  - Create Acceptable Use Policy that everyone must read and agree to follow
- 7.4 Communication
  - Determine communication needs (what, when, who, why, ....)
- 7.5 Documented Information
  - check teh doc including all the mandatory docs for ISO Mandatory and non-mandatory documents and records for ISO 27001.pdf
  - Write document control procedures (Availability and suitability for use, Protection, Storage and preservation, Version control,....)
## Clause 8
- 8.1 Operational planning and control
  - Implement clause 6.

  - Have a change management process.
  - Identify and control outsources processes
- 8.2 Information Security Risk Assessment
  - Conduct risk assessment
  - Document the results of your risk assessment
- 8.3 Information Security Risk Treatment
  - Implement risk treatment plan.
  - Document the results of risk treatment.
